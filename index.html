What is VisualMath?

VisualMath is an educational platform designed to enhance the learning experience by providing clear and interactive visualizations of mathematical concepts. The site offers tools and resources that help students understand and explore topics covered throughout college-level mathematics. By transforming abstract mathematical ideas into visual formats, VisualMath aims to support students in developing a deeper comprehension of mathematical principles and improve their underlying problem-solving skills.
