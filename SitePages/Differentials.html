Dynamical Systems and Differential Equations:

Dynamical Systems and Differential Equations delve into the study of change and how systems evolve over time. From modeling population growth to understanding physical phenomena like fluid flow or electrical circuits, these topics are critical for solving real-world problems. Our tools and materials are designed to simplify the process of understanding how equations describe the dynamics of systems and how solutions can predict future behavior.
